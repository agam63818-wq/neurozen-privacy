Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026
NeuroZen is a brain training app built for everyone. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains clearly what data we collect (spoiler: almost none), and how your information is handled.

Information We Collect Minimal

NeuroZen does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.

  • No name, email, or phone number is collected
  • No location data is collected or tracked
  • No payment or financial information is collected
  • No device identifiers are collected or stored

Data Stored on Your Device

All your game progress, brain scores, streaks, and settings are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your phone and is not sent to any server.

  • Brain Score and game history
  • Day streaks and XP progress
  • App preferences and settings

Third-Party Services

NeuroZen does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not share any data with third parties.

Internet Permission

NeuroZen works fully offline. The app may request internet access only for initial loading as a Progressive Web App (PWA/TWA). No data is transmitted during this process.

Children's Privacy

NeuroZen is suitable for users aged 6 and above. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, children's privacy is fully protected by design.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
