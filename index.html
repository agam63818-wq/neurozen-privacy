Privacy Policy

🔒 Privacy-First App
Last updated: June 22, 2026  ·  Effective Date: June 22, 2026
NeuroZen is a brain training app built for everyone. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent about how our app works. This Privacy Policy explains clearly what data we collect (spoiler: almost none), how it is used, and your rights regarding your information.

Information We Collect Minimal

NeuroZen does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app. We have designed NeuroZen from the ground up to work without collecting personal data.

  • No name, email, or phone number is collected
  • No location data is collected or tracked
  • No payment or financial information is collected
  • No device identifiers, advertising IDs, or unique identifiers are collected or stored

Data Stored on Your Device

All your game progress, brain scores, streaks, and settings are stored locally on your device only, using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your phone and is not sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

  • Brain Score and game history
  • Day streaks and XP progress
  • App preferences and settings
🗑️ Deleting your data

Since all data lives only on your device, you can permanently erase it at any time by clearing the app's storage/cache from your device settings, or by uninstalling NeuroZen.

Third-Party Services

NeuroZen does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting, or tracking tools. We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties, because no personal data is ever collected in the first place.

Internet Permission

NeuroZen works fully offline. The app may request internet access only for initial loading as a Progressive Web App (PWA/TWA) or for occasional app updates. No personal data is transmitted during this process.

Data Security

Although NeuroZen does not collect or transmit personal data, we still take reasonable technical measures to ensure that any information stored locally on your device remains safe from unauthorized access, in line with your device's own operating system security features.

Your Rights & Choices

Because NeuroZen does not collect, store, or process any personal data on our servers, there is no personal data for us to access, export, or delete on your behalf. You remain in full control of any data stored on your own device at all times, and can remove it by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

NeuroZen is suitable for users aged 6 and above. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other user. Since no personal data is collected from anyone, children's privacy is fully protected by design.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback about this Privacy Policy or NeuroZen, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.
